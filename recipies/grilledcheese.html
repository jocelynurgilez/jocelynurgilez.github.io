First recipe: Grilled Cheese

The best recipie to make when in a hurry or when your a teenager is grilled cheese.

You will need:

  • 2 slices 1/2 of Pullman or other white bread
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2 ounces thinly slices American cheese or cheddar (about 4 slices)
  • freshly ground black pepper
  • Optional: tomato soup

Steps:

  1. Spread mayonese on top of the bread slices (this helps the bread not burn and turn golden)
  2. Get a small skillet (nonstick, perferably) to heat over medium heat.
    • Slide in half of butter
  3. When it melts, place 1 slice of bread, mayonnaise side down, in skillet
    • top with cheese; season with pepper
  4. Top with second slice of bread, mayonnaise side up
    • wait about 4 minutes
  5. Turn sandwich and add remaining butter to skillet
    • Press down on sandwich to encourage even browning and to help melt cheese. Be gentle, donâ€™t smash it
  6. Cook until second side is golden brown and cheese is melted
