Third Recipe: Ceasar Salad

A ceasar salad is a healthier option. If you want to make something quick and low on carbs, this is the perfect choice!

You will need:

  • 3 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of bread crumbs
  • 6 cups of chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1/2 of finely grated pasmesan cheese
  • Optional: cooked chicken
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 2 medium garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp of fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 tbsp of salt
  • 1/2 cup of avocado oil or any other oil
  • 2 tsp of worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 tsp of freshly cracked black peper

Steps:

  1. On a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat until glistening
  2. Add in the panko bread crumbs and toss to combine
  3. Reduce heat to low and cook
    • stirring often until breadcrumbs are golden brown
    • about 3 min
  4. remove from skillet and set aside
  5. In a blender, add:
    • egg yolks
    • garlic
    • 1 tbsp of lemon juice
    • salt
  6. Blend until ingredients are combined
    • If dressing gets too thick, add:
      • the remaining lemon juice
      • worcestershire sauce
      • black pepper
      • blend until combined
  7. On a big bowl, mix:
    • lettuce
    • dressing
    • half of the greated parmesan cheese
    • Optional: chicken
  8. top with:
    • leftover cheese
    • bread cumbs
    • lefftover peper
